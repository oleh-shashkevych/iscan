About

Our goal is to provide manufacturers and developers with long term, innovative, safe, reliable, and flexible automation solution that raises production yield and improved space utilization.

Future-Proof Solution For Manufacturing - In 1997, I-Scan’s straight line design concept of implementing robots directly on the main-line to replace mechanical side-legs with stacking machines appeared to be radical. Today on-the-fly systems are implemented in almost any glass producers in North and South America, Europe, China, and the Middle East. In 2023 a revolutionary optimizer package was introduced to the market, one that can improve line yield, save energy, and ensure production stability.

Improving the bottom line for budling developers with ROBOX - Our innovative, reliable, simple ROBOX solution for building storage reduce your capital investments, improve customer experience, and improve safety.

I-Scan’s heavy payload MAVEN AGV - Our innovative, flexible, reliable, simple solutions reduce your capital investments, increase your manufacturing flexibility, and improve safety.
